Not into romance? Kiki's Delivery Service might just be the perfect movie for you! It's a wholesome story about self discovery and making friends along the way. 		Genres: Fantasy • Comedy • Adventure

Kiki's Delivery Service is about a 13 year old independent witch who took up a delivery job for her village's tradition of witch training. She soon becomes challenged with her insecurities and starts to lose her powers, she must find away to overcome her self-doubt and get her powers back.

Howl's Moving Castle is one of the most popular Studio Ghibli movies and it's popular for a reason. I would be lying if I said I didn't enjoy this movie. In between the lines you can find philosophical observations on how humans tend to behave, all while also allowing us to imagine a world of selflessness. 		Genres: Fantasy • War • Drama • Romance • Adventure

Howl's Moving Castle is about a girl named Sophie, and unconfident woman who isn't all too keen on romance and living in a fantasy of being picked up by some man. When she is cursed with an old body by a spiteful witch, her only chance of breaking the spell lies with a self-indulgent yet insecure young wizard and his companions in his legged, walking castle.
